Chapter 3 · Workflow Automation

7 Essential Automations
Every Business Needs

Each workflow connects your tools so they work together automatically — with no coding required for most of them.

01 · 30 minutes to build
New Customer Onboarding
TRIGGER: Purchase complete
Welcome email, product access, customer segment tagging, CRM record creation, and Slack notification — all fire simultaneously the moment payment clears.
⚡ Recommended: Zapier or n8n
02 · 15 minutes to build
Review Request Pipeline
TRIGGER: 7 days post-purchase
Personalized review request sent at Day 7. Automated follow-up at Day 10 if no response. Respondents tagged as brand advocates automatically.
⚡ Recommended: Email platform natively
03 · 1–2 hours to build
Content Repurposing Pipeline
TRIGGER: New blog post or video published
AI extracts key points, formats for each social platform — Twitter thread, LinkedIn post, Instagram carousel, TikTok script — and schedules everything automatically.
⚡ Recommended: n8n + OpenAI
04 · 1–2 hours to build
Lead Scoring
TRIGGER: Subscriber opens, clicks, or visits
Score increases with each engagement. Interest tags applied automatically. When a threshold is hit, a high-intent sales sequence is triggered — no manual intervention needed.
⚡ Recommended: ActiveCampaign or n8n
05 · 30 minutes to build
Abandoned Cart Recovery
TRIGGER: Checkout started, not completed
Hour 1: recovery email. Hour 24: second email with incentive. Hour 48: urgency email. A three-touch sequence running entirely without you — recovering revenue around the clock.
⚡ Recommended: ThriveCart or Shopify
06 · 1–2 hours to build
Financial Dashboard
TRIGGER: Daily or weekly schedule
Pulls revenue and refund data from Stripe, updates a live Google Sheet dashboard, and emails a weekly summary — so you always have a clear financial picture.
⚡ Recommended: Make or n8n
07 · 1–2 hours to build
AI Content Generation
TRIGGER: New idea added to Notion
Topic sent to OpenAI API for a first draft, saved to Google Docs, editing task created in your project tool, and team notified — a full content workflow on a single trigger.
⚡ Recommended: n8n (best AI integration)
"Start with #1 and #2. Add one automation per week."

Chapter 4 · Customer Support

Handle 90% of
Inquiries Automatically

The Support System Pyramid routes inquiries to the right level — so human effort is reserved only for what actually requires it.

45%
Level 1 — Self-Service
FAQ page and knowledge base handles common questions automatically. Zero human involvement.
30%
Level 2 — AI Chatbot
Tidio, Intercom, Crisp, or a custom n8n + OpenAI bot handles dynamic questions and troubleshooting.
13%
Level 3 — Templates
Standard issues resolved by a VA using saved reply templates. Response target: 4 hours.
7%
Level 4 — Human Escalation
Complex issues, refunds over $50, and VIP interactions. Owner or senior VA only. 24-hour target.

Chapter 10 · Implementation

Your 90-Day
Automation Roadmap

Weeks 1–2
Audit & Foundation
  • Complete 7-day time audit
  • Categorize: Eliminate, Automate, Delegate, Focus
  • Set up automation platform
  • Automate sales funnel
  • Build FAQ with top 20 questions
  • Create first 3 SOPs
Weeks 3–4
Automate & Document
  • Build customer onboarding automation
  • Set up review request workflow
  • Configure help desk or chatbot
  • Automate financial tracking
  • Write all marketing & support SOPs
  • Set up content repurposing
Weeks 5–8
Hire & Delegate
  • Post first job listing (social VA)
  • Paid test tasks with 3–5 candidates
  • Onboard first hire with SOPs + Loom
  • Implement weekly management rhythm
  • Hire second specialist
  • Build content approval workflow
Weeks 9–12
Optimize & Scale
  • Audit all automations for failures
  • Structured team performance review
  • Add advanced automations
  • Delegate next product creation
  • Build weekly metrics dashboard
  • Plan next quarter with new capacity
